Patricia's Toronto

Personality packaging for hand crafted chocolate collection.

Patricia’s Toronto is an artisan bake shop in the heart of Toronto. We created unique packaging for their 2020 Valentine’s Day collection.

Process

Taking a crafted approach requires creativity, patience and planning from beginning to end. The goal was to create a customer experience that would be flavourful and personality packed from the momen customrs pick up a chocolate bar until the very last bite.

We analyzed best sellers and sales patterns from past campaigns to help us determine flavours and a visual direction for the packages. From there, we brainstormed product names, illustration concepts and campaign themes.
