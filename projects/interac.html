Interac

One of the most trusted financial brands in Canada, facilitating transactions between merchants and consumers.

See It Live

Interac sought to make their website accessible, modern and engaging to both businesses and everyday people.

Process

The goal of this project was to create a component based site that could be easily maintained by the Interac team. During her time at TWG, Kristiana created a series of web components, made UX recommendations and collaborated on the art direction with team members of Toronto advertising firm, Zulu Alpha Kilo.

To start, we completed a UI audit of the pre-existing Interac website to highlight any inconsistencies, determine key components and uncover weaknesses in the pre-existing visual system.

Outcomes & Design

We presented our final design system as a component guide. The goal is to help remove any blindspots that the Interac developers, copy writers and internal team may have as they update the website in the future. It contains notes on topics such as development functionality, interactivity, word and character counts and design notes.

Colour Scheme

One of our biggest challenges was working with an inflexible and limited color palette. We applied colours to feel light and airy to help Interac seem approachable and friendly.
