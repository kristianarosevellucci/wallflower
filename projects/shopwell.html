Shopwell

Shopwell is an e-commerce design studio in Victoria, BC. We made illustrations for their website.

See It Live

Shopwell’s team is made up of designers, developers, and copywriters who have each launched their own online business. Wallflower designed visual assets to help customers connect with Shopwell’s story.

Process

Shopwell’s mission is to humanize the e-commerce experience.

They help simplify the ecommerce process for overwhelmed business owners who are new to operating an online store front.

The team began thinking about the types of assets that would work best for the Shopwell’s team, as they already had strong design elements and copy.

Outcomes & Design

Inspired by Shopwell’s company culture, Wallflower created a series of illustrated characters that brought their team to life.

The teams felt it was important to keep the illustrations simple - leading to the black and white line style.

The goal was to create a narrative depicting how e-commerce businesses still rely on human-to-human interactions.

Results
Shopwell is thriving as a design shop and the Wallflower team couldn’t be happier for them!
