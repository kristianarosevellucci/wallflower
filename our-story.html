Oh, hey there!

I'm Kristiana, one of the makers here at Wallflower Studio.

Like so many designers, I’ve always been more on the quiet side, often preferring to to listen and observe rather than being centre stage. When I was 19, I decided to try freelancing and eventually went on to work at a product company, startup, and an agency: each of which allowed me to look at design, business and teamwork from different perspectives.

After collaborating with clients at various stages in their business, I realized that working with small business owners with causes near and dear to them made my heart grow three sizes ( literally!-- just kidding). I also realized that my ability to listen, observe and draw connections are essential for brand building and product positioning.

And so just like that, Wallflower Studio was born!

We’re now a small creative crew that uses narrative driven design, interactivity, and carefully crafted content to create brands and website that hit you right in the feels.
