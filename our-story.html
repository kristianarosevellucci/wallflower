Wallflower is a branding and web design studio based in Toronto.

Our creative crew uses narrative driven design, interactive assets, and carefully crafted content to create brands and websites that hit you right in the feels.

We believe design should be about personality.

Connecting with your audience is about more than buzzwords and a flashy logo. Your brand needs to speak for itself across all channels, drive your strategic decision making, and feel authentic.

We’re not afraid to get intimate and create something true to you.

The ability to listen, observe, and draw connections are essential for brand building and product positioning - this is why we chose the name Wallflower.

It doesn’t matter if you are starting at the beginning or have been in business for 50 years - we’ve built and launched products, websites, and brands for businesses of all shapes, colours, and ages (from startups to large production companies and agencies).

Once our work is done, we will happily take our place behind the scenes - cheering you on as the world watches you bloom.
